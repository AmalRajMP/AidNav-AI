PM-KISAN (Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi)

The PM-KISAN scheme provides income support of ₹6,000 per year to eligible farmer families.

Eligibility:
- Small and marginal farmers
- Indian citizens
- Valid Aadhaar

Benefits:
- ₹2,000 is transferred every four months directly to the beneficiary's bank account.
